    We are Iwan fruit

    Mr. Iwan had always wanted to have his own fruit shop, but things didn't go smoothly at the beginning. He started by selling fruits from a small cart on the roadside, but he faced stiff competition and found it hard to make ends meet. One day, while surfing the web, he had a bright idea. He noticed that there were no online produce shops in Indonesia, and he saw an opportunity to grow his business. He spent a lot of time researching and learning about website design, online marketing, and e-commerce.

    After months of hard work, he finally launched his online fruit shop, Iwan Fruit. It turned out to be a great hit with customers. People loved the convenience of being able to order fresh fruit from their homes, and his business grew rapidly.
